Our Story

All big things have small beginnings...🌱

  • First sight

    February, 2016

    In Winter 2016, when Anh (bride) was still a student at the University of Utah and I (groom) was on my first business trip from VietNam to Salt Lake city Utah, we were both invited by a common friend to a Vietnamese New Year party. And that's where we first met each other and became friends.

  • Falling for each other

    June, 2017

    In Summer 2017, I had another chance to see Anh in my second business trip to Utah. During a few short weeks, we realized that our friendship was special and we couldn't resist from taking it further to become lovers. Despite all the odds that we were living in two different countries at the time, we started a long distance relationship and promised to work on building our future together since then.

  • Fulfilling our promises

    June, 2018

    Preparing for studying abroad, at one time I had to deal with 2 engineering jobs and A Graduation Record Examination literature review, but hardworking was paid off. Summer 2018, I came to U.S to get higher education in Electrical and Computer at University of Utah; and of course, to be able to hold Anh's hands again. We both had worked non-stop to support each other and to bring our future paths closer.

  • And here our adventure begins...

    Now

    We are extremely grateful to have found each other, to share hopes and dreams, friends and family, and to be able to walk side-by-side wherever we go.. 'til the end of Earth.
